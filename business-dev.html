Business Development

Our Mission


The world is is filled with countless things to explore and experience. But most people are content with doing the same things over and waiting for something to happen to them. We aim to change that by using data to deliver precise personalized event recommendations directly to your fingertips.



The Challenge


UEvents strives to provide users with personalized event recommendations that provide them with a plethora of exciting options to tap into at any given time.


As we expand into more places and take on more users and events, we need to maintain a scalable architecture that successfully stores and manipulates the data we collect.


So how will we ensure that when a user opens up our platform we are able to provide them with a stellar experience - to provide them with stellar event recommendations in a heartbeat?



The Role


Through continually aggregating event information and interacting with our users, we've stored a tremendous amount of data that we are only just beginning to tap into. This treasure trove of opens up vast possibilities for us, providing us with a truly unique business opportunity. Your role is to help us unlock our potential.


Build a company.You will be working closely with the CEO and spearheading the business operations of the team. You'll also have the opportunity to build your own team as we grow.


Help shape our revenue model. As a young startup transitioning from a cool app to a business, we need to nail down our business model if we truly want to succeed. Help us explore potential financial avenues and to decide which ones worth pursuing.


Play a key role in outreach. A big part of success comes from making sure we have the content that users want. This means that we need to make sure we know about all the best events happening around town, which means that we need to know the right people. Help us find and partner with these individuals.


Wear many hats. There are plenty of things to be done and you will dipping your hands in multiple projects at once. From working with the Development team to create new features to sourcing potential clients - you will get the authentic startup experience.



About You


You’re as enthused as we are about fundamentally changing local event discovery and you can't wait to get started.


Flexible and fast learner. You're ready to jump right into anything from making cold calls to drafting business plans. You're attentive to detail, yet you're focused on the big picture. You're also able to learn new skills, tools and processes on the go.


Embrace the unknown. You get shit done. You’re also a self-starter - you can take a project and run with it, but also know to ask for help when you’re stumped. You're unfazed and quite frankly, excited, at the challenge of finding creative solutions to difficult problems with limited resources.


Organization is your strength. You'll be juggling multiple projects and working across different teams - communication and organization will be your best bet to ensure that all deadlines and milestones are met.
