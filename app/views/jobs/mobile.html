Mobile Developer (Android or iOS)

Our Mission


College is about meeting new people and sharing unforgettable experiences together. We help students create these long-lasting memories by showing them events both on and off-campus that we think they’ll love.



The Challenge


UEvents strives to provide users with personalized event recommendations that provide them with a plethora of exciting options to tap into at any given time. The popularization of mobile devices opens up a world of possibilites for us to explore. Taking advantage of unique mobile features like push notifications and geo-location, we could take personalization to the next level.



The Role


Design and build new features from ground up: crafting UIs with designer and building Android or iOS applications with Java or Swift.


Practice disciplined software engineering, including automated testing, code reviews, and writing beautiful, pragmatic code.


Communicate closely with users, collect feedback/metrics, and rapidly iterate on product.


Test and optimize features and interfaces to ensure quality experiences for users on all devices and browsers.


Be flexible, grow, and learn fast in a startup environment



About You


You have a passion for working on live projects that impact real people. We care more about experience and enthusiasm than degrees and awards


Previous experience in building Android or iOS apps.


You are a self-starter. You can take a project and run with it, but also know to ask for help when you’re stumped.


Quality and cleanliness of code matters to you. You understand that taking shortcuts now don't pay off in the long run.


You have good taste in design and mobile UI/UX.



Apply Now!
